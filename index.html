If You Decide to Leave

Zero lock-in. Here's exactly what happens — no fine print.

Day 0 You tell us you're leaving. We cancel your subscription at period end. No questions, no retention calls.
Days 1-14 Your Claw runs in read-only mode. Full access to your data, configs, and conversation history.
Day 14 We create a full server snapshot. Your droplet is stopped.
Days 15-30 Choose your handover: download all files (configs, skills, memory, data) or transfer the server to your own DigitalOcean account. Both free.
Day 30 All your data is permanently deleted from our systems. Clean break.

Why we're explicit about this: Most providers say "you own everything" but don't explain what happens. We do. Because trust isn't built on vague promises.
